Pitch your Thoughts

Abstract

Pitch Your Thoughts is a theme-based paper presentation competition designed to bring together innovative minds from diverse technical domains. This platform provides students and researchers with the opportunity to showcase their knowledge, creativity, and problem-solving skills through impactful research papers and technical presentations. The goal of Pitch Your Thoughts is to spark curiosity, encourage critical thinking, and promote knowledge sharing among budding innovators while bridging the gap between academia and real-world applications.

Minute to Win

Abstract

Minutes to Win ⏱️ is a high-energy coding showdown designed to test your logic, speed, and wit. Get ready for mind-twisting puzzles, shuffled codes, and mysterious QR hunts that keep you guessing till the end. Every second counts as you decode challenges, outsmart the twists, and race against time. Expect surprises, dares, and pure coding adrenaline. It’s not just about coding — it’s about staying sharp under pressure. Step in, think fast, and prove you have what it takes to win every minute!

Grab, Solve, Climb

Abstract

Grab, Solve, Climb is a dynamic three-round team competition designed to challenge participants’ mental agility, logical reasoning, and teamwork. The event unfolds through progressively intense stages — from answering rapid-fire questions to solving puzzles and culminating in an interactive quiz-based Snake & Ladder game. Each round tests quick thinking, strategy, and coordination under time constraints. The competition encourages collaboration and sharp intellect, rewarding the most strategic and alert teams that successfully climb their way to the top.

Coordinator: Mr. Sowmiya [9791339165]

Dice of Destiny

Abstract

Dice of Destiny is an engaging and unpredictable team event that blends creativity, improvisation, and daring challenges. Spread across three thrilling rounds — Movie Clues Swap, Sell the Property, and Dice of Destiny — the competition pushes participants to think fast, act confidently, and embrace spontaneity. Every dice roll determines fate, bringing new twists and challenges. With a mix of fun, performance, and audience engagement, the event promises laughter, excitement, and unforgettable moments as teams battle for victory.
